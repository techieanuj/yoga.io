About Serenity Yoga

Our Philosophy

At Serenity Yoga, we believe that yoga is more than just physical exerciseâ€”it's a holistic practice that unites mind, body, and spirit. Our approach integrates traditional yoga teachings with modern understanding of wellness to create a transformative experience for our students.

Founded in 2010 by Master Instructor Elena Rodriguez, Serenity Yoga has grown from a small studio to a community of over 500 practitioners. We're committed to making yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of age, fitness level, or background.

Our certified instructors bring diverse expertise from various yoga traditions, ensuring that our classes are authentic, safe, and effective. We continually update our teaching methods to incorporate the latest research in mindfulness and movement science.

Elena Rodriguez, Founder & Lead Instructor

With over 15 years of teaching experience and certifications in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Iyengar yoga, Elena brings a wealth of knowledge to Serenity Yoga. Her journey began in India where she studied under master teachers for several years before bringing her expertise back to the community.

Elena's teaching philosophy centers on mindful movement, proper alignment, and the therapeutic benefits of yoga. She has helped hundreds of students overcome injuries, reduce stress, and discover greater joy in their lives through yoga.
